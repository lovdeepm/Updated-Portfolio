About Me

My name is Lovdeep Mander, I was born in Yuba City, California. When I was 5 I moved to Livingston, California, about 2 hours south, where I went to school and graduated High School. After high school I attendated CSU Sacramento, where I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Operations Management and Marketing.

After graduating I had an opportunity to work at Foster Farms, where I have been the last 10 years. I have held a few different postions inside the production plant. I started off at a Supervisor overseeing 90 employees, currently I am a Department Supervisor for the shipping/receving areas. Currently I have 4 supervisors and 120 employees I oversee.

I started the coding bootcamp program in November of 2018, it is something that interested me very much, so I am planning a career change. Currently we are working on HTML and CSS to create a website.
